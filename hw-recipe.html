How to bake Banana Bread


Ingredients:

  • 3 Bananas
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 Cup of flour
  • 1/2 Cup of Sugar

Instructions:
  1. Mash up 3 bananas in a bowl so there is no clumps
  2. Add in 1 egg and mix
  3. Add in 1 cup of flour and mix
  4. Add in 1/2 cup sugar and mix
  5. Spray a bread pan with non-stick cooking oil and pour contents into pan
  6. Bake in preheated over at 350 Degrees for 55 min
  7. Enjoy!
